5. Payouts

Payouts are issued when the pool finds a block. The payout structure is as follows:

  • The miner who finds the block receives a 0.3125 BTC finder bonus (10% of the block subsidy).
  • The 100 miners who submitted the highest-quality shares that round each receive 0.0275 BTC — a total of 2.75 BTC (88%) split equally.
  • 2% is retained as the pool fee (0.0625 BTC).

All payouts are sent directly as coinbase outputs to the Bitcoin addresses used as mining usernames. No manual withdrawal is required. The pool does not hold user funds.

Miners ranked #101 and below in a given round receive no payout for that round. The leaderboard resets after every block.

6. Fees

The pool fee is 2% of the block subsidy — exactly 0.0625 BTC per block at the current subsidy level. No additional fees or withdrawal charges apply.
